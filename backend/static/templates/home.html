Discover projects based on their commit activity

Git Leaderboard is a place where you can share your git projects' stats and see how they compare against others. Stats are submitted via the downloadable commit checker which sends them to the central leaderboard.

See who the top committers are

We analyze authors' commit history to compare projects a variety of ways.

  • Most total commits
  • Most authors
  • Most commits by any single author
  • Lead author with highest percent
  • Average commit count per author
  • Highest Average Commits
  • Lowest standard deviation by author
  • Lowest coefficient variation by author
  • Recently submitted
